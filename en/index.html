Foundation Breaking Barriers Without Limits for Humanity

We are a non-profit foundation, whose main focus is to support the Salvadoran population, through various social programs that contribute to a better development and human quality.

Salvadoran youth inspire us to keep going, fostering good works together with families and friends.

Read more

We are a family

Our foundation treats everyone as one family, including those we help and help in the future. That is why we want to support and safeguard the well-being of all people who require our help. Following our values and main axes, we will help in sectors such as education, home, health and the environment. Do you want to be part of it?

Mission

To create a better quality and to be a standard of human development open to new learning techniques for the urban and rural communities in which the foundation has a presence. Promoting the development and social inclusion of the people we serve generating new opportunities. We guarantee access to better programs and projects in our main axes.

Values

Solidarity

We build a development approach based on human rights, aimed at its fulfillment and eradicating inequality in the communities with which we work.

Commitment

We act committed to the fulfillment of our mission and vision and with integrity, as a fundamental principle of our actions.

Excellence

We ensure the achievement of our objectives and seek the greatest impact of our actions through the optimization of our capabilities.

Participation

We promote democratic and participatory principles as a necessary means for social transformation and as an inherent end of development.

Transparency

We base our work on honesty, mutual responsibility and maximum access to information, as pillars of social and economic accountability to our collaborators, donors, beneficiaries and society in general.

Unity

We believe in teamwork and collaboration with other organizations and institutions to enhance synergies and improve the performance of our work.

Dedication

We want to be useful and serve others, giving the best of each one. This vocation prevails in relations with those who are part of the Foundation and with those who cooperate outside of it. At the same time, we demand that all projects and programs be effective.

Results of your support

What do we do?

The foundation has been working for eight years in the areas of Education, Health, Environment, Social Development, Family Sustainability. With these axes we contribute to a process of progressive change, agile and operational in each of the projects that are carried out.

We cover different areas of social and community development to contribute to our country through the training of children, youth and adults, improving the quality of life of the people who benefit from the projects.

How do we work?

We work in the western area and have subsidiaries in other cities Texistepeque, Nejapa, Nahuizalco, Los Angeles California and with the various projects we offer greater opportunities that allow rural communities to raise their quality of life, at the same time we seek to improve their social development and family economy through professional training workshops in which they learn a trade to become sustainable families.

Project ECOESCUELA (2022)

An Eco school is an educational center in which a process of environmental improvement is developed, through research through an audit and subsequent correction of the detected deficiencies, which implies an improvement in educational practice.

Food Delivery Project

In response to the needs of the local communities focused on the Family Sustainability axis we make visits to the vulnerable families that we serve from different areas of Metapan delivering food and vitamins thanks to the support of different institutions that donate different products that help in LA food and health of families.

Literacy Program

Within the Education Axis we are developing and implementing the literacy program in communities of the municipality of Metapan.

Progress of the literacy process in the community cazuelejas canton 'El Rosario'. The foundation continues to carry out and strengthen development education projects for communities.
